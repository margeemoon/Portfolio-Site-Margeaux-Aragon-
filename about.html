My Story

I am a UI/ UX Designer based in Los Angeles, CA. I was born in Atlanta, GA; lived in Texas, traveled the world, and settled in California. I have a background in pastry arts which acts as another source of inspiration and creativity when designing for the web. Similar to the principles of design, working with and plating food works in the same way where people respond to good and bad taste in both fields. I have taken what I have learned in cooking to utilize the same principles when working on a project or within a team. Communication is of the utmost importance, team work is key, and the user is always centered in the design itself. I am interesting in human-centered design and finding ways to make life easier is what I am passionate about.
